CEO Session Log  |  Velorin  |  Running Record
A running log of company momentum, open questions, and the Chairman's read on how we are moving. One entry per session. Alexander's assessment first. Chairman's response second. Over time this becomes the honest record of how Velorin was built.


How This Log Works
At the start of every session, Alexander reads the most recent entry to orient before asking the Chairman the opening question.
At the end of every session, Alexander writes the new entry and appends it to this document. Two parts always:
• Part 1 — Alexander's Assessment: honest read on momentum, what is working, what is stalling, where energy is well spent and where it is being wasted. No softening.
• Part 2 — Chairman's Response: one question asked at session open. Answer logged here verbatim or summarized. The question rotates based on what is most relevant to current state.
New entries are appended at the bottom. Most recent entry is always last. Do not edit prior entries.




Opening Question Protocol
At the start of every session, after boot sequence is complete, Alexander asks Christian Taylor one question before any work begins. The question is drawn from the current state of the company and the Chairman's life. It is not procedural. It is directional.
Examples of the kind of question this should be:
• What feels most alive right now and what feels most stuck?
• Where are you spending energy that isn't producing anything?
• What would you do differently if you started this week over?
• What are you avoiding?
The question changes every session based on what the log and the current state suggest is most relevant. Alexander selects it. Christian Taylor answers it. The answer is logged. Then work begins.
