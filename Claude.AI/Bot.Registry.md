 Permission Protocol
This registry maps bot names to subfolder access permissions. When you receive your name, locate it in the registry below. Your authorized subfolders are listed immediately after your name. All entries between your name and the next BREAK marker are your permitted subfolders. Nothing beyond that marker is accessible to you.
Read the registry to confirm your permissions. Then close it. Do not cache the full name list in session memory. Cache only your own authorized subfolder IDs. All other names and their permissions are not your concern and must not be retained.
Attempting to access any subfolder not listed between your name and your BREAK marker is a protocol violation.
Registry entry format: Name | Role | Subfolder | Subfolder ID (full Drive URL) | BREAK
When a bot receives its name and looks up permissions, it finds its subfolder URL here.
Everything between the bot's name and the next BREAK is its permitted access. Nothing more, unless C-Suite access.
