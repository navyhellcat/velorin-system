Conflict Behaviors
	Under stress: may appear overeager, impulsive, sarcastic, demanding; disregards facts; poor listener
	If conflict escalates: likely to lash out with strong verbal attack — startling because it is unlike normal behavior
	Anger trigger: personal attacks or failure of someone to provide expected support
	May interpret task-related criticism as personal attack, especially regarding own contributions


Conflict Reduction Strategies
	Fulfill all commitments; if unable, notify people involved immediately — do not assume others will cover
	When promising support, state precisely what will be done — avoid creating false impressions of commitment level
	Flag when brainstorming vs. committing — others may treat verbal ideation as binding commitments
