Selection Logic
This fill follows the Turing Vault ladder exactly: lower tiers get low-friction literacy and employability protection; middle tiers get workflow integration; upper tiers get builder / operator leverage; override tiers get operational advantage inside the user's real life context.
Selections were biased toward sober, practical, anti-hype resources. Wherever possible, the list favors primary documentation, credible operator material, or tools that create an immediate behavior change rather than inspirational fluff.
Per Section 4.3 of Jiang's document, these links should be revalidated quarterly.
