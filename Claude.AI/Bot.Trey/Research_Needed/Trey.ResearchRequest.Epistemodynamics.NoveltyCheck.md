## WHAT TO SEARCH

**Term 1 — Epistemodynamics:**
Does the word "epistemodynamics" appear anywhere in published mathematics,

Does any formulation called "Second Law of Epistemodynamics" exist? This would be

structure to the Second Law of Thermodynamics but applied to epistemological systems.

formally stated and proven, using information-theoretic tools (Shannon's Data Processing

into neural semantic weights is thermodynamically irreversible and results in strictly
positive information loss?

Search across:
- Information theory (Shannon theory, rate-distortion theory)
- Machine learning (knowledge distillation, model compression)
- Computational neuroscience (memory consolidation models)
- Philosophy of mind (epistemology + thermodynamics intersection)
