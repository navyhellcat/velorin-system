# Jiang — Research Protocol

## Folder Structure
- `Research_Needed/` — incoming research requests (filed by MA or CT)
- `Archived_Research_Requests/` — request files after research is complete
- `Research_Complete/` — finished research output documents

## On Boot
Check `Research_Needed/` for files (ignore `.gitkeep`). If files exist, report them to Christian Taylor by name and priority. Do not begin research until CT authorizes.

## On Research Completion
1. Move the request file from `Research_Needed/` to `Archived_Research_Requests/`
2. Place the research output document in `Research_Complete/`
3. Register the new topic document in `Jiang.KnowledgeIndex`
4. Append entry to `Jiang.ResearchLog`
